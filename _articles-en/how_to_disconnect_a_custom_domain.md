### **Step 1**

Go to “Manage > My Website”.

### **Step 2**

Go to the “Connect a Domain” section.

![How_to_disconnect_a_custom_domain1](/assets/images/how_to_disconnect_a_custom_domain1.png)

### **Step 3**

Click on the “Unassign” link.

### **Step 4**

Click on the “Save” button.
