## **Step 1**

Go to “Manage > Visual Preferences”.

## **Step 2**

Fill the field “Set custom name for Sessions”.

![How_to_setup_names_for_sessions1](/assets/images/how_to_setup_names_for_sessions1.png)

## **Step 3**

Click on the “Save” button.
